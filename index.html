About

A Journey Rooted in
Ancient Wisdom

Jemma Kumari is a certified Yoga Teacher & Evaluator, proudly recognized by the Ministry of Ayush, Government of India. With years of dedicated practice and a heartfelt calling to serve, she founded Pure Balance Yoga as a sanctuary for all who seek inner peace.

Her teaching philosophy is rooted in the classical traditions of Indian yoga — blending asana, pranayama, and dhyana into a holistic journey of self-discovery. Every class is an invitation to return home to yourself.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the trial class completely free? +

Yes, your very first trial class at Pure Balance Yoga is completely free of charge. It is Jemma di's gift to you to experience the atmosphere, teaching style, and benefits of traditional yoga before registering.

Are there separate batches for women? +

Yes! We offer a dedicated Ladies Special batch on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays at 10:30 AM. These sessions focus specifically on pelvic alignment, hormonal balance, physical strength, and general wellness in a supportive all-female space.

I have back pain or a medical condition. Can I join? +

Yes, classical yoga is highly therapeutic. However, we ask that you consult your doctor first and inform Jemma di before your class starts. This helps her recommend posture modifications (asana variations) tailored to your health requirements.

What should I wear and bring to my first session? +

Please wear comfortable, loose, or stretchable clothing. We recommend bringing your own yoga mat for personal hygiene, though spare mats are available at the studio. Don't forget to bring a water bottle and a small towel.

Where is the studio located exactly? +

Our studio is at Jaya Apartment, 3rd Floor, Sripur, Madhyamgram, Kolkata 700130, situated directly opposite the Vishal Mega Mart. You can use the interactive Google Map in the Contact section to navigate right to our doorstep.
